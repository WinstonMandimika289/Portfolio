Synopsis

This documentary indulges into the lives of current U.K university students and there experineces with the class b drug. We will explore the different attitudes towards the substance and aim to give the viewer enough information to make their own judgement.

We aim to give an unbiased opinion and we urgue you to remember, we DO NOT condone the use of ANY illegal substances. The characters involved are willing participents and also on going university students so we have decided to protect their identity.
